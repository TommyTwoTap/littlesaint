E-MAIL BROADCASTS

The Board of Commissioners initiated an e-mail broadcast system in early 2008. Information regarding meetings, events on the lake, updates to the website and other topics of interest are periodically sent to all e-mail addresses provided by interested property owners. A history of the messages is then maintained on this website for future reference.

To be included in the broadcast system, please send your e-mail address to tritter3@verizon.net.
